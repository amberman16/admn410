Delivery Policies

We have very strict delivery policies. The customer must be home at the time of the delivery. There is a flat rate of $20 to have your product or pet delivered to your home. You must also sign a non-disclosure contract before delivery.

Return Policies

We currently have a 30 day return policy if you are unsatisfied with your purchase. If you are not satisfied, you are guaranteed your money back. Pet supply returns must be in brand new condition. Further, you may not make returns on pets.
